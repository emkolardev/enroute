Authoring Interactive Media

Emily Kolar - Fall 2015

  • Why?

    I love traveling, and thus take road-trips as often as possible. If I don’t know how to get somewhere, Google Maps routing feature is my best friend. It would be awesome and super useful if the app were more versatile and if the navigation didn’t have to be canceled and restarted anytime I want to find the nearest Starbucks or Shell station, and even better if it would search places that are along my current route.

  • Core Audience

    This is somewhat vague, but a) smartphone users, b) adventurers, and c) those that desire a simpler and/or more streamlined navigation experience. Though this isn’t technically a user group, it does indicate what will be crucial to the design of the app.
